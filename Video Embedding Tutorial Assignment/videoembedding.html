Video Embed Tutorial

Welcome to our video embedding tutorial!

In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to embed a service-hosted video into your website.

Step 1: Find the Video

Go to the video hosting service and locate the video you want to embed.

Step 2: Access Embed Options

Look for the "Share" or "Embed" option below the video player. Click on it to access the embed options.

Step 3: Configure Embed Settings

Adjust any settings available, such as video size, autoplay, or related videos. Copy the generated embed code.

Step 4: Create HTML File

Open your HTML file using a text editor. Insert the copied embed code where you want the video to appear.

Step 5: External Style Sheet

Create a separate CSS file to style your HTML content.

Step 6: Apply Styles

In the CSS file, add styles to enhance the layout of your page.

Step 7: Test Your Page

Open your HTML file in a web browser to test the layout and ensure the video is embedded correctly.

Step 8: Refine as Needed

Adjust styles and content as needed to achieve the desired look and feel.
